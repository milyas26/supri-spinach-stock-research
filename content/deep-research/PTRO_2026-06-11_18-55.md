## 1. Profil Emiten

**PT Petrosea Tbk** (IDX: PTRO) adalah perusahaan multi-disiplin yang bergerak di bidang **jasa pertambangan, EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction), dan jasa minyak & gas bumi**.

- **Didirikan:** 21 Februari 1972 (sebagai PT Petro-Sea International Indonesia)
- **IPO:** 1990 — perusahaan rekayasa & konstruksi pertama di IDX
- **Kantor Pusat:** Wisma Barito Pacific, Jakarta Barat
- **Karyawan:** ~6,431 orang
- **Website:** petrosea.com

**Bisnis Inti (4 Segmen):**
1. **Mining Services** — kontrak tambang terbuka (overburden, drilling, hauling), pit-to-port. Revenue US$389.25M (43.9%)
2. **Engineering & Construction** — EPC multi-disiplin untuk oil & gas, infrastruktur, industri. Revenue US$379.75M (42.8%)
3. **EPCI Offshore Oil & Gas** — subsea pipeline, platform construction (via Hafar Group). Revenue US$32.87M (3.7%) — SEGMENT BARU
4. **Services & Coal Sales** — supply base, port services, perdagangan batubara. Revenue US$84.59M (9.5%)

**Anak Perusahaan:**
- PT Karya Bhumi Lestari, PT Rekakarsa Karya Nusantara, PT Cristian Eka Pratama (coal reserves 164.1M tons)
- **HBS Group** — mining & construction di Papua Nugini & Australia
- **Hafar Group** — EPCI offshore (supporting oil & gas)
- Petros Solutions Australia Pty. Ltd.
- PT POSB Infrastructure Indonesia (offshore supply base di Sorong)

**Keunggulan Kompetitif:**
- 54+ tahun pengalaman di Indonesia
- Pit-to-port integrated solutions
- Minerva Digital Platform (Industry 4.0)
- Remote Operations Center (ROC) — real-time monitoring
- Sertifikasi internasional (ASEAN Corporate Governance Scorecard)
