## 1. FTSE Country Classification — March 2026 Interim Review

📎 Sumber: [FTSE Russell — March 2026 Interim Announcement (7 April 2026)](https://www.lseg.com/en/media-centre/press-releases/ftse-russell/2026/ftse-russell-announces-results-march-2026-semi-annual-country-classification-review-equities-fixed-income) | [PDF Lengkap](https://www.lseg.com/content/dam/ftse-russell/en_us/documents/country-classification/ftse-interim-country-classification-review-2026.pdf)

### Watch List (per Maret 2026)

Hanya **1 negara** di Watch List:

- **Mesir** — possible demotion: Secondary Emerging → Frontier
  - Tinggal 1 saham eligible di FTSE Emerging Index per akhir 2024 (minimum 2)
  - Reformasi ekonomi + inisiatif Egyptian Exchange mulai menaikkan likuiditas → 1 saham baru masuk eligibility Maret 2026
  - **Nasib ditentukan setelah 30 Juni 2026** (cek ulang minimum securities count + investable market cap)

### Reklasifikasi Dikonfirmasi

| Negara | Dari | Ke | Efektif |
|--------|------|----|---------|
| **Nigeria** | Unclassified | Frontier | **21 Sep 2026** |
| **Vietnam** | Frontier | Secondary Emerging | **21 Sep 2026** (multi-tranche s/d 2027) |
| **Greece** | Advanced Emerging | Developed | **21 Sep 2026** |

**Nigeria:** Di-unclassified sejak Sep 2023 karena repatriasi modal macet. Sekarang FX queue udah clear, investor institusi asing udah bisa tarik modal tanpa delay material.

**Vietnam:** Circular 08/2026/TT-BTC resmi membentuk Global Broker model + non-prefunding framework. Broker global dan lokal tinggal finalisasi perjanjian bilateral. FTSE "satisfied" dengan progress. Implementasi multi-tranche untuk akomodasi kapasitas pasar lokal.

### Pasar dalam Pemantauan (Tanpa Watch List)

**Indonesia — Secondary Emerging (TIDAK BERUBAH):**

> FTSE Russell continues to closely monitor capital market integrity reforms in Indonesia... FTSE Russell is not considering Indonesia for inclusion on the Watch List.

Inisiatif reformasi yang dicatat FTSE:
- Enhanced shareholder disclosure (data kepemilikan di atas 1%)
- Expanded investor classification categories
- Minimum free float requirements
- High Shareholding Concentration (HSC) list
- Enhanced market surveillance tools

**Korea Selatan — Developed (TIDAK BERUBAH):**
- Short-selling ban dicabut Maret 2025, tapi "Short sales permitted" masih rated "Restricted"
- Masalah pre-funding settlement saat index rebalance — broker harus commit capital berkali-kali
- Investment Alert/Risk list masih jadi beban operasional, terutama buat mid-cap

**Oman — Frontier:**
- Memenuhi 9 kriteria Secondary Emerging, tapi kurang jumlah saham (perlu 3 Large/Mid + 5 total, sekarang 2 Mid + 3 Small)
- Akan direview lagi September 2026

## 2. Indonesia & FTSE — Kronologi & Status Terkini

📎 Sumber: [FTSE Russell FAQ — Treatment of Indonesian Securities v1.3 (Juni 2026)](https://www.lseg.com/content/dam/ftse-russell/en_us/documents/policy-documents/ftse-faq-document-treatment-of-indonesian-securities-in-ftse-russell-equity-indices.pdf) | [Indonesia Index Notice — June 2026 Review (13 Mei 2026)](https://research.ftserussell.com/products/index-notices/home/getnotice/?id=2619601)

### Timeline

- **29 Jan 2026** — OJK umumkan komitmen peningkatan integritas & transparansi pasar modal
- **5 Feb 2026** — IDX rilis rencana reformasi
- **9 Feb 2026** — FTSE **PAUSE** semua index changes untuk saham Indonesia (kecuali corporate actions tertentu)
- **Maret 2026** — Index review Maret untuk Indonesia **ditunda total**
- **April 2026** — OJK/IDX implementasi: shareholder data >1%, HSC list, investor classification enhancement
- **7 April 2026** — FTSE konfirmasi Indonesia tetap Secondary Emerging, tidak masuk Watch List
- **13 Mei 2026** — FTSE umumkan treatment Juni 2026: review parsial
- **Juni 2026** — Review berjalan terbatas, full resumption ditargetkan September 2026

### Apa yang DILAKUKAN di Juni 2026

- ICB (Industry Classification Benchmark) updates
- Quarterly share updates (tanpa buffer 1%)
- Quarterly free float **decreases only** (tanpa buffer 3%)
- Large/mid/small/micro-cap changes dari spin-offs (deletion kalau di bawah exit threshold)
- Exclusion lists tetap jalan (ESG, Shariah, dll)
- Untuk indeks dengan annual/semi-annual review Juni: **deletion** dari FTSE4Good, Blossom, Environmental Markets indices tetap applied

### Apa yang MASIH DITUNDA di Juni 2026

- Additions (termasuk IPO) ke indeks
- Quarterly free float **increases**
- Full index re-ranking (peringkat ulang saham berdasarkan market cap)

### Alasan FTSE Menunda

Mengutip langsung FAQ:

> "...the accurate determination of free float percentages is currently uncertain, and implementing index changes at this stage may lead to adverse turnover for index users."

Penundaan sesuai Section 2.4 — Exceptional Market Disruption dari Index Policy FTSE Russell.

### Outlook September 2026

> "FTSE Russell will continue to monitor market developments in Indonesia closely and remains engaged with local market authorities. Further decisions on index treatment, including potential resumption of full index re-ranking, will be considered in advance of the September 2026 index review and communicated in due course."

## 4. Implikasi buat Indonesia

### Positif
- Indonesia **tidak masuk Watch List** — tidak ada risiko downgrade ke Frontier dalam waktu dekat
- Reformasi OJK/IDX diakui FTSE sebagai langkah positif — enhanced shareholder disclosure, HSC list, investor classification
- Juni 2026 review berjalan parsial (bukan full freeze seperti Maret) — sinyal progres
- Target September 2026 untuk full resumption — kalau reformasi berjalan lancar

### Risiko
- Ketidakpastian free float masih jadi concern utama FTSE — ini basis perhitungan bobot saham di indeks global
- Kalau September 2026 masih ditunda lagi, ini bisa jadi red flag untuk investor institusi asing
- Reformasi pasar modal Indonesia masih dalam pengawasan ketat — belum "lulus" dari FTSE
- Jangan lengah: dulu **Nigeria** dari Frontier ke Unclassified cuma karena repatriasi modal tersendat; **Pakistan** dari Secondary Emerging ke Frontier karena market cap turun

### Next Key Dates
- **30 Juni 2026** — Cutoff data Mesir (Watch List reassessment)
- **September 2026** — Review indeks Indonesia (potensi full resumption)
- **21 September 2026** — Efektif: Nigeria ke Frontier, Vietnam ke Secondary Emerging, Greece ke Developed
- **6 Oktober 2026** — **FTSE Annual Country Classification Announcement** (INI PENTING — next Watch List update)

## 5. Glossary

| Istilah | Penjelasan |
|--------|------------|
| **Developed** | Pasar maju (US, UK, Jepang, dll) — 25 negara |
| **Advanced Emerging** | Emerging kelas atas (Brazil, Taiwan, South Africa, dll) — 10 negara |
| **Secondary Emerging** | Emerging kelas dua — **Indonesia di sini** bareng India, China, dll — 12 negara |
| **Frontier** | Pasar perbatasan (Vietnam, Nigeria, Kenya, dll) — 28 negara |
| **Watch List** | Daftar negara yang sedang dipertimbangkan untuk reklasifikasi naik/turun |
| **Free Float** | Persentase saham yang benar-benar bisa diperdagangkan publik (non-strategis, non-pengendali) |
| **GEIS** | FTSE Global Equity Index Series — indeks acuan global FTSE |
| **ICB** | Industry Classification Benchmark — sistem klasifikasi industri FTSE |
| **HSC List** | High Shareholding Concentration — daftar saham dengan konsentrasi kepemilikan tinggi (baru di Indonesia) |
